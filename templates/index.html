How real are your fears?

We all feel overwhelmed with negative emotions from time to time. Fear of the unknown or fear of future often takes a toll on us. But have we ever wondered how real our fears are? This app gives us an opportunity to journal our thoughts about an imagined future reality and compare it with the actual reality. That way we can understand and learn how to take our thoughts -NOT so seriously.

Journal your thoughts and get a reality check later

This feature allows you to journal your thoughts about an event in future. And then lets you journal again when the time for the event is over so you can compare. For example, you are worried about presenting your pitch to investors on 10th Nov. You go online and write in as much detail about your worrying thoughts for that day. Then you go and actually give your pitch on the destined day and write about how it actually went. Finally, you see both your thoughts side by side. The imagined ones and the real ones.
